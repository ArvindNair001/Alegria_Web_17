Wanderlust

"Wanderlust" is this year's theme and inspiration for "Alegria- The Festival Of Joy". This theme was crafted from the never-ending thirst to see the world and explore unknown possibilities and adventure. Participants at Alegria are given the opportunity to showcase their talent, experiences and creativity while awakening their inner traveller. This is the 4th year of Alegria and the festival has only been getting bigger and better every year with the popularity and participation spreading to colleges and youth across India. To add to this, the natural beauty of our campus and the infrastructure, combined with the warmth of Alegrian hospitality makes the Alegria experience something that you definitely don't want to miss.
Join this joyride from February 2nd to February 6th with thousands of friends from various colleges across the country. Let's experience Talent, fun, Life and Music!!
